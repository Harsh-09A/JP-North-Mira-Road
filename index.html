Overview

JP North Mira Road

JP Infra's seventh tower, The Palace, captures everything that makes JP Infra homes stand out. Whether it's the intelligent use of space, the vibrant social infrastructure, or the inspiring amenities, everything is designed to make residents' lives easier. JP North Garden City, located near Podar International School in Vinay Nagar, Mira Road, Mumbai, is a thoughtful creation by JP Infra. Spread across 22.00 acres, this community comprises 12 buildings housing 5000 units, offering a mix of 1, 2, and 3 BHK configurations to cater to diverse living needs. JP North Garden City is a commitment to providing a warm and welcoming home. With prices starting at a reasonable 69.99 lakhs, the project ensures affordability without compromising on quality. What sets it apart is the seamless integration of modern amenities with the serenity of nature. Residents can relish a balanced lifestyle, surrounded by lush greenery and top-notch facilities. JP North Garden City reflects JP Infra's sincere effort to create not just houses, but spaces that resonate with the essence of human living.
